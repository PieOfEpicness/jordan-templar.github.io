Jordan Templar

Writer

Jordan Templar is an up-and-coming screenwriter based in Naarm (Melbourne.) They have been writing for many years, and are ready to take the next step in their career. Their work is inspired by the things they love: video games, books, film and TV, their friends, family, and cat Lionel.

Jordan is a believer in the power of storytelling, dedicated to bringing diversity into every area of film, TV and video game creation. They are neurodiverse, queer and disabled. They are currently studying at RMIT (Bachelor's of Creative Writing), and also have an Advanced Diploma of Professional Screenwriting. They are interested in writing for film, TV and video games.
